About

Built by someone who got tired of applying and hearing nothing back.

I built GET ME PAST ATS because I know what it feels like to apply to job after job and never hear back. A lot of people are qualified, willing to work, and ready to learn, but their resume never makes it to a real person.

Most job seekers do not know how to tailor a resume to a job post. They do not know what keywords are missing, what dates look confusing, or what red flags a hiring system may catch before a recruiter ever sees them.

This tool was created to help regular people stop applying blind, fix weak spots, track applications, and prepare better before they apply.

    Privacy

    Simple privacy promise

    GET ME PAST ATS uses account information and saved application data to help users access their tracker. Resume text and job posts are used to show results inside the tool.

    The goal is simple: help job seekers improve applications. Do not paste Social Security numbers, driver’s license numbers, banking details, or other sensitive personal information into the analyzer.

    Terms

    No interview guarantee

    This tool can help improve resume wording, catch missing keywords, and organize job applications, but it cannot guarantee interviews, job offers, or hiring decisions.

    Always keep your resume honest. Use the suggested wording as a starting point and edit it to match your real experience.
