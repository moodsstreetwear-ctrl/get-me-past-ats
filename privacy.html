Privacy Policy

GET ME PAST ATS was built to help job seekers compare resumes to job posts, catch missing keywords, review red flags, and track applications.

Information you provide

When you use the tool, you may provide your name, email address, resume text, job descriptions, company names, job titles, pay details, application status, and notes you save in your tracker.

How the information is used

Your information is used to show resume analysis results, save applications to your account, help you return to your tracker later, and improve the product experience.

What not to enter

Do not paste Social Security numbers, driver’s license numbers, banking information, medical records, passwords, or other sensitive personal information into the analyzer or tracker.

Account data

If you create an account, your saved applications are connected to your account so you can come back later from another device.

Data sharing

GET ME PAST ATS does not sell your resume data or saved application data. The goal is to help users apply smarter, not to sell their personal job search information.

Security

No website can promise perfect security, but GET ME PAST ATS is designed to limit saved data to each signed-in user’s account.

Contact

For questions about privacy, use the contact information provided by GET ME PAST ATS when available.
